About Us

FPS is Biggest gaming event which will have multiple games ranging from Valorant to CODM. Participants can join with pre-made teams or interact and make teams on the day of the event. The whole event will be managed on the discord platform. FPS will have multiple sub-events like a Valorant Tournament on PC to a Call Of Duty Mobile Tournament for mobile gamers.

Participants can register for multiple sub-events and participate in all of them! Prerequisites for the FPS Event are: minimum specification PC’s for people registering for PC events, all the games downloaded before-hand, and a discord account.
