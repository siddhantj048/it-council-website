Welcome to Thinkathon!

Virtual Hackathon!

About

This event is a virtual hackathon that will be conducted in two stages.

In the first stage, contestants will have to present their ideas and solutions for the topics given to them. Those who qualify for the first stage will have to take part in the second stage.

The second stage will consist of a product designing challenge where contestants can code or can design a 2D/3D model of the base of their idea. The team with the best idea and execution wins!

Rules

  • You can participate solo or as a group with max 4 members.
  • You will be given 2 days to prepare a presentation on the topic prior to the event.
  • The presentation should be done through a PPT.
  • You will have to turn on your microphone and camera to present
    (if a member fails to turn on his camera, it will result in points deduction).
  • Judges will decide who will advance to the second round based on the presentation.
  • The rubics for the selection will be given prior to the event.
  • The top 3 teams will advance to the second round where they will have to make the product design based on
    the topic they choose.
  • The product design can be a basic website(HTML page) or a basic 3d render of the product.
  • The top 3 teams will be given 5 hours after their selection to prepare for the second round.
  • Each team/person will be given 5 mins to present their PPT to the judges.
  • The event will be held live on zoom.
